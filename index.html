Crystal Collector

Game Play

The game starts with a random number.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add that crystal's number to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, and all numbers are reset after each round.
